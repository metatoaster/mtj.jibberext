Credits
=======

A few people have found the services of the bots that utilized this
module to be useful that they found it worthwhile to donate some things
back as a source of encouragement for building/fixing things in here.

digital charon
    500M ISK donated after some major improvements were made to the
    ``mtj.jibberext.web.RandomImgur``.

TownSaver
    350M ISK donated after the ``mtj.jibberext.utility.Relay`` was added
    for relaying messages from disused channels to another target.

Joe D'Trader
    250M ISK donated after the ``mtj.jibberext.web.VideoInfo`` was added
    so TEST general chat has YouTube title reporting function returned
    as the other bot that did it went MIA for quite some time.
